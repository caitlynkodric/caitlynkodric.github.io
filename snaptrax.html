The project was created at NHacks '16 presented by NSpire in London, ON. You snap a picture, you get a track. SnapTrax is the best way to connect your memories to music. Imagine your life as a photo album, now imagine your life as a soundtrack. SnapTrax connects these ideas to enhance the way you remember moments in your life by putting music to your memories.
The website allows one to upload a URL or local image to receive a new song. To choose the song, an API is used to anazlye the image to retrieve tags which then match the tags of specific songs. The website will also show a feed of previous uploaded images, with hopes of being specific to each user.
Skills Accquired:

  • ‣ Used Agile development; utilized Clarifai API to collect tags from uploaded images or URLs, outputting a SoundCloud song
  • ‣ Developed website application using HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, and Github
